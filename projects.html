Projects

Coperni x Nike

For the communication design project, we had to help the company achieve specific goals with an idea and make an advertisement for the company. I did this project for my favorite brand Coperni and thought collaboration with the global brand Nike would be a good idea. So I did the campaign for Coperni and Nike.

I sketched out and designed the product of Coperni x Nike in Procreate. I made the new logo for the campaign (also in Procreate). For the final advertisement, I used photography, using myself as a model. I used Photoshop to make it look like I was holding the product.

Pressage Magazine Cover/Mockup

This is a “Presage” fashion magazine cover and mockup. This is a magazine by French photographer Maxime George. I met him and his team in October, and he gave me a one-time job creating the magazine cover and the mockup for their social media. I used different tools for this work including Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator.

The New Yorker Cover

For the Illustration class, we had to sketch out the cover of one of the most popular magazines - “The New Yorker”. I did my sketch digitally through Procreate, using a picture from my birthday as inspiration.

Dove Product

For the communication design class, we had to create a unique product for the well-known brand Dove that would support women and improve their overall wellbeing. My creative idea was to make a cream to alleviate pain during the menstrual cycle. The goal was to let women all around the world enjoy their usual lives and feel strong and beautiful in their hardest time. I used Adobe Photoshop and Procreate for this project.

Me2

For the InDesign class, we had to create a magazine for our target audience, including the layout, articles, and illustrations. I focused on Feminism, covering topics like women’s education, women in politics, and women’s suffrage in the United States. I used Adobe InDesign and Illustrator for this project.
